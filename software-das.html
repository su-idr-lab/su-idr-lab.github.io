Legacy Software

DAS 2D/3D is a legacy compliant-mechanism analysis platform focused on the design and synthesis of planar and spatial rigid-body and compliant mechanisms. The public site positions the software as a dedicated environment for compliant mechanism analysis and related Java-based design tools.

The suite is important historically because it represents an early and sustained investment in mechanism-design software that links analysis, synthesis, and educational use.
