VRMDS is a virtual-reality-based mechanism design and simulation environment intended to support immersive interaction with engineered mechanisms. Its associated paper and tutorial video position it as a research platform for interactive visualization and simulation rather than a conventional standalone CAD package.

This software reflects an early interest in how virtual environments can support design understanding, spatial reasoning, and simulation-centered engineering workflows.
