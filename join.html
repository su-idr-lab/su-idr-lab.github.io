Open positions and collaboration

IDR Lab welcomes motivated researchers interested in intelligent engineering design, robotics, mechanism science, and simulation.

Prospective students

  • PhD students interested in engineering design, robotics, learning, and kinematics.
  • MS and undergraduate researchers looking for project-based research experience.
  • Applicants with backgrounds in mechanical engineering, robotics, computer science, applied mathematics, or related fields.

How to apply

  1. Add your contact email and preferred subject line.
  2. Request a CV, transcript, and short statement of research interests.
  3. Optionally ask applicants to identify which research themes align with their interests.

Collaboration
