Research Focus

We develop computational tools and automation pipelines that support mechanism synthesis, engineering geometry generation, and design analysis for rigid-body and compliant systems.

This theme emphasizes software-driven design workflows that reduce manual engineering effort and provide reusable platforms for mechanism design, kinematic synthesis, and compliant-system analysis.

  • DAS-2D: design and synthesis of planar rigid-body and compliant mechanisms.
  • DAS-3D: design and analysis of spatial compliant mechanisms.
  • Synthetica: kinematic synthesis of spatial linkages.
