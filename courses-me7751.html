ME 7751 Compliant Mechanisms

This graduate course covers compliant mechanisms that gain mobility through elastic deformation rather than conventional joints. The course is organized around two core modules: pseudo-rigid-body models for planar compliant systems and screw-theory-based approaches for spatial compliant mechanism design and analysis.
